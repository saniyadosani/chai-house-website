About Us

The Chai House HTX is a handcrafted chai and coffee concept founded in Houston, bringing the warmth and authenticity of South Asian tea culture to the heart of Texas. We specialize in traditional favorites like Kashmiri Chai and Rose Chai, alongside innovative creations such as Pistachio Coffee, Ras Malai Matcha, and our signature Meetha sweets.
